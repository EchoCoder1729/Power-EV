Bluetooth Connectivity and Push Notifications

We'll utilize Bluetooth to establish a connection and send push notifications for every 5% decrease in the charge level once the charge level of the EV drops to 30%.

Software for Charging Stations

We will be developing a software for the charging stations so that we can track the real time occupancy data of the connectors. It will help us to collaborate with the charging stations more efficiently.

Trip Planner

Using this feature, EV users will be able to plan their outstation trips in advance without worrying about their EV charging needs. Our app will automatically book the charging slots by estimating the distance the EV can travel on single charge.
